About Mikron Industries

Transforming the Extrusion Industry

With over 30 years of experience, we proudly lead the way in the design and manufacture of high-quality extruded profiles for building products and window and door systems.

Our Story

For over 30 years, Mikron Industries has been dedicated to producing innovative extruded profiles and solutions. Our automated manufacturing processes ensure exceptional quality, efficiency, and sustainability, making us a trusted partner for clients worldwide.

Vision

We envision a future where our extruded profiles set the standard for quality and sustainability in the construction industry. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction drives us to exceed expectations in every project.

Core Values

At Mikron, we are committed to excellence, sustainability, and innovation. We pride ourselves on our ability to adapt to changing market needs while maintaining the highest standards of quality and service.

As a leader in the extrusion industry, we serve clients in 14 countries, delivering unmatched expertise and support through our advanced manufacturing processes and dedicated team.
